Law & Order: Special Victims Unit

1999, TV-14, 23 Seasons

In the criminal justice system, sexually based offenses are considered especially heinous. In New York City, the dedicated detectives who investigate these vicious felonies are members of an elite squad called the Special Victims Unit.
